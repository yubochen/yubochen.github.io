Demonstration System
I led the development of the multi-faceted knowledge system CogNet and its associated toolkit CogKit. Aimed at researchers and developers, it offers a suite of knowledge extraction, computation, and services, encompassing a variety of computational functions such as knowledge extraction, knowledge representation learning, knowledge querying, knowledge-enhanced text representation, knowledge updating, and knowledge services.
